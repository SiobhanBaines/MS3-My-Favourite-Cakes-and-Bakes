About Our Favourite Recipes


Every family has a "Grandma's old recipe book", full of family favourites that have been handed down and cooked by many generations.


What better way to bring the family together than to bake a tea time treat that brings back memories of your childhood. We all have our favourite treats we love to bake.


Get the children involved. There's always something easy for them to make, eat and have fun in the kitchen.


You can create your own profile and add your favourite recipes to share with others or just have a look around and see what other people love.

Ready to join Our Family Favourites?


Click the register button and set up your username.


Decide which of your favourite recipes to add.


Click the add recipe button to begin adding your recipe. If you have a photograph of the finished product please upload it. We love to see home cooking. If not, don't worry you can always add a description of it in the Notes section.


If you make a mistake, you can always change it later


None of the categories that are set up relate to your recipe? We have a page to add new categories.


Not ready to join the fun? That's ok too. Click on the recipes tab or one of the images on the carousel and enjoy.
